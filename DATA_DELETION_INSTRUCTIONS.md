Data Deletion Instructions for Heritage Trivia

Heritage Trivia uses Facebook Login to provide a seamless experience. We do not save your personal data on our servers. According to Facebook policy, we have to provide a User Data Deletion Callback URL or Data Deletion Instructions URL.

If you want to delete your activities for Heritage Trivia, you can remove your information by following these steps:

Go to your Facebook Account’s Setting & Privacy. Click "Settings".

Look for "Apps and Websites" and you will see all of the apps and websites you linked with your Facebook.

Search and Click "Heritage Trivia" in the search bar.

Scroll and click "Remove".

If you wish to completely erase any residual account data from our system, please send an email to contact@babafish.com with your deletion request, and we will process it within 48 hours.
