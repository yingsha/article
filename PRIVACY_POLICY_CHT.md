## CCPA and CPRA Privacy Notice (California Privacy Rights)

Under the CCPA (amended by the CPRA), California residents have specific rights regarding their personal information.
* **The right to notice:** You have the right to be notified which categories of Personal Data are being collected and the purposes for which the Personal Data is being used.
* **The right to request / the right to know:** Under CCPA, You have the right to request that We disclose information to You about Our collection, use, sale, disclosure for business purposes and share of personal information.
* **The right to delete:** You have the right to request the deletion of Your Personal Data.
* **The right to opt-out of the sale or sharing of Personal Data:** We do not sell Your personal information.
